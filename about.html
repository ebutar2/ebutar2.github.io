About

This website will serve as a fan site for the Star Wars universe. In the database, there is a function where user can generate random characters from the Star Wars universe. These characters will include information such as Name, Height, Mass, Hair Color, Skin Color, Eye Color, Birth Year, and Gender. It will also include plot summary about the original triliogy, prequel triliogy, and sequel trilogy.
